Welcome to CALI, A Linguistic Portal to Proficiency in Spanish. CALI is a tool that provides its users a path to develop their reading proficiency in Spanish.

Search for Texts in Spanish by Reading Proficiency Level Using CALI

Using CALI, teachers, learners, and researchers can search for texts in Spanish by reading proficiency level. CALI makes this possible using techniques from natural language processing and machine learning that are informed by results from decades of psycholinguistic research on reading comprehension.

Users can also enter in their own texts to have CALI calculate their reading level as well as analyze them for other important linguistic measures useful in second language learning and teaching.
